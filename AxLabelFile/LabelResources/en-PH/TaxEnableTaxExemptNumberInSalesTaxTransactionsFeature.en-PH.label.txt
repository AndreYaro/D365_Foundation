Summary=This feature controls how tax exempt numbers are populated in VAT transactions. If it is enabled, the tax exempt number in a VAT transaction will be copied from the corresponding document header, e.g. Sales order, Purchase order, or Free text invoice. Otherwise, the tax exempt number will be copied from the customer/vendor master data.
