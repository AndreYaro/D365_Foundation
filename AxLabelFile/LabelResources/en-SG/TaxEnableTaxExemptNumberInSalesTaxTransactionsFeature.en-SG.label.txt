Summary=This feature controls how GST registration numbers are populated in GST transactions. If it is enabled, the GST registration number in a GST transaction will be copied from the corresponding document header, e.g. Sales order, Purchase order, or Free text invoice. Otherwise, the GST registration number will be copied from the customer/vendor master data.
