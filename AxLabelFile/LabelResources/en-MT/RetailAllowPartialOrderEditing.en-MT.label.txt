AllowPartialOrderEditingFeatureTitle=Edit partially fulfilled orders in Point of Sale
 ;Label for RetailAllowPartialOrderEditingFeature
AllowPartialOrderEditingHelpText=This feature will allow users of the Point of Sale (POS) application to edit orders originated in store or e-Commerce channels that have one or more lines that may have already been fully or partially Fulfilled. This feature does not enable editing of orders originating from a call centre channel through POS, regardless of the order’s Fulfilment state. Enabling this feature will also replace the order status information shown in POS with a calculated detailed status for orders displayed in the Recall Order and Order Fulfilment operations.
