Summary=This feature controls how tax codes are populated in VAT transactions. If it is enabled, the tax code in a VAT transaction will be copied from the corresponding document header, e.g. Sales order, Purchase order, or Free text invoice. Otherwise, the tax code will be copied from the customer/supplier master data.
