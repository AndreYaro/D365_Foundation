Summary=This feature controls how VAT numbers are populated in VAT transactions. If it is enabled, the VAT number in a VAT transaction will be copied from the corresponding document header, e.g. Sales order, Purchase order, or Free text invoice. Otherwise, the VAT number will be copied from the customer/supplier master data.
